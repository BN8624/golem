The deterministic tactics kernel with five stacked cards. Cards 1-4: Mana Shield + ANOMALY, ranged attack, terrain (Wall/Conductive), and enemy unit types (Hardened/Glass/Resonant). Card 5 (루트 맵, this card): a campaign layer that chains several battles into one route. A scenario may carry an optional route — an ordered list of the battles that follow the first — and the shared action list is consumed across the whole route. When the current battle's enemies are all defeated and the hero still lives, the run advances to the next battle: the hero's hp/atk/mana/anomaly_dmg carry over, the position resets to [0,0], and the next battle's enemies and terrain load in. The route is won only when the final battle is cleared; the hero dying anywhere is defeat; running out of actions mid-route is finished. The kernel contract and the exact 5-field output are unchanged; route data and the battle index stay internal, and battle advancement lives in updateState so the engine loop and the base modules never change. The nineteen prior worlds (no route) reproduce byte-for-byte; three new worlds exercise a two-battle win with carried-over hp, an advance that then runs out of actions, and a defeat in the second battle.

The deterministic, hero-only tactics kernel with six stacked cards. Cards 1-5: Mana Shield + ANOMALY, ranged attack, terrain (Wall/Conductive), enemy unit types (Hardened/Glass/Resonant), and the route map campaign. Card 6 (상태이상, this card): Corrosion, a damage-over-time effect. If the hero carries an optional hero.corrosion = {dmg, duration}, a ranged attack also poisons its target with Corrosion (re-applying refreshes the duration). At the end of every hero action — after combat, before battle advancement and the win/loss check — each living corroded enemy takes the corrosion damage (doubled by Glass), then its duration decays and the status clears at zero. A corrosion tick can finish an enemy and so clear a battle, advancing the route. The kernel contract and the exact 5-field output are unchanged; corrosion and the status field stay internal, and battle advancement plus ticking both live in updateState so the engine loop and base modules never change. The twenty-two prior worlds (no corrosion) reproduce byte-for-byte; three new worlds exercise a DoT kill over several actions, Glass doubling both the ranged hit and the corrosion tick, and a corrosion kill that advances a route.

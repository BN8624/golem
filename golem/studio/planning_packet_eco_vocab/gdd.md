- **Player actions**
    - Setup: Input world size, entity starting positions, and initial energy. State changes to `RUNNING`. Failure: Negative values or positions outside world size. Log: "World initialized."
    - Step: Advance one tick. State updates via the tick loop. Failure: None (deterministic). Log: "Tick [N]: [Summary of populations]."
- **Entities**
    - **Predator**: Moves toward the nearest Prey. Gains energy by eating Prey. Reproduces at threshold.
    - **Prey**: Moves away from the nearest Predator. Reproduces at threshold.
    - **Movement Rules**: 
        - All moves are 1 cell. 
        - Ties in distance (e.g., prey at index 2 and 8 while predator is at 5) are broken by moving toward the lower cell index.
        - Boundaries (0 and worldSize-1) are hard walls; entities cannot move outside.
- **Win/Lose conditions**
    - Termination: Game ends if only one species remains (Extinction) or the `tickCap` is reached.
- **NON-GOALS**
    - Randomness/Stochasticity (no `Math.random`).
    - 2D movement or complex terrain.
    - Real-time clock or interactive mid-game input.
    - Graphical rendering.

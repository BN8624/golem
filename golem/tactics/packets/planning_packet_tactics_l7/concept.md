The deterministic, hero-only tactics kernel with seven stacked cards. Cards 1-6: Mana Shield + ANOMALY, ranged attack, terrain, enemy unit types, route map, and Corrosion DoT. Card 7 (밸런스/난이도, this card): an optional balance config that tunes difficulty by data. A scenario may carry config = {atkMult, recMult, anomMult} (each defaults to 1, persists across a route): atkMult scales the hero's outgoing base damage (melee, ranged, corrosion ticks) before enemy-type modifiers, anomMult scales the anomaly base before Conductive and Glass, and recMult scales the damage the hero receives before the Mana Shield (and the Resonant self-damage). All multipliers floor to integers. The kernel contract and the exact 5-field output are unchanged; config stays internal and applies only within the hero's action resolution, so the engine loop and base modules never change. The twenty-five prior worlds (no config) reproduce byte-for-byte; three new worlds show recMult turning a win into a defeat, atkMult letting a weak hero one-shot, and anomMult amplifying a small anomaly into a clearing blast.

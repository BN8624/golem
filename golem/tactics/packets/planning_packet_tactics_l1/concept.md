A deterministic, turn-based tactical combat prototype (the frozen tactics kernel) extended with the first card mechanic: the hero's "변칙검술" Mana Shield. Incoming attack damage is absorbed by hero.mana before it touches hero.hp. When that shield ruptures — mana crosses from above zero to zero or below in a single attack — an ANOMALY discharges, dealing anomaly_dmg to every orthogonally adjacent living enemy. The kernel contract (REQ-001~011) and the exact 5-field output are unchanged; mana and anomaly_dmg are internal state only. The six base worlds (no mana) reproduce the kernel byte-for-byte; three new worlds exercise absorb-without-rupture, rupture-with-survivor, and rupture-kill-splash.
